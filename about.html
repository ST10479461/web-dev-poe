Our Story

Homemade Delights began in our home kitchen, where weekend baking turned into a neighbourhood tradition. Today, we’re a small team of bakers who believe the best memories start around the table. We focus on simple recipes, quality ingredients, and warm service. From big cakes to small cakes, donuts, your child’s first birthday cake or a loaf for Sunday lunch, we’re honoured to be part of your moments. With branches in Fordsburg and Norwood, we’re proud to be part of everyday celebrations and family moments.

Mission & Vision

We believe in quality, community, and the simple joy of freshly baked treats.
